About the ingredients

The base of every tin is organic, grass-fed and green-finished tallow — rendered slowly to preserve the naturally occurring vitamins and fatty acids that closely mirror what's found in human skin. We blend it with a short list of organic plant oils to soften the texture and round out the nourishment.

  • Grass-fed, green-finished tallow Slowly rendered from pasture-raised cattle, rich in vitamins A, D, E and K.
  • Organic plant oils A simple blend chosen for absorption and a soft, non-greasy finish.
  • Nothing else No synthetic fragrance, fillers, or preservatives — just what your skin needs.
